About Calterra Homes

Calterra Homes is a residential builder serving the Greater Sacramento area, focused on custom homes and accessory dwelling units (ADUs). We emphasize clear scope, disciplined planning, and consistent communication from preconstruction through completion.

Calterra Homes is operated by Alex Fong, a California Licensed General Building Contractor (B).

About Alex

I’ve been in construction for over 10 years, starting with residential remodels in the Tahoe area. I later attended California State University, Sacramento, where I studied Construction Management with a minor in Business.

While in school, I worked with MJB Homes, Inc. and gained hands-on construction management experience across the full residential building process—design coordination, planning, permitting, construction, and final inspections. That experience continues to shape how I approach projects today.

Why Calterra Homes

I started Calterra Homes because I genuinely love the construction industry and saw an opportunity to improve the experience homeowners have when working with contractors.

Poor communication, unclear scope, and unrealistic expectations create unnecessary stress during what should be an exciting process. Our goal is to provide a building experience that feels organized, transparent, and professionally managed from start to finish.

How We Do Our Best Work

We do our best work when projects are thoughtfully planned and expectations are clearly defined from the beginning. Clear communication, realistic planning, and early decision-making allow projects to move efficiently and reduce unnecessary stress.

Every project begins under different circumstances, but the goal remains the same: bring structure, clarity, and professionalism to the process so homeowners feel informed and confident throughout the build.
